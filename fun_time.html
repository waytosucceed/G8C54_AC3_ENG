Idioms

Elbow Room: Enough space to move around freely or to be comfortable.
Example: "The new office gives us plenty of elbow room to work without feeling cramped."

Explore All Avenues: To try every possible way to achieve something or solve a problem.
Example: "The detectives decided to explore all avenues to find the missing person."

Eat Humble Pie: To admit one's mistake and apologise or accept a situation with humility.
Example: "After the argument, John had to eat humble pie and apologise to his friend for being rude."
